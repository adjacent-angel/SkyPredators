Sky Predators Bird Abatement Service is built on the principles of using the natural, extremely effective, ancient art of falconry to rid your property of nuisance birds through the use of highly trained falcons.

How does it work?

Falconry-based bird abatement is the use of highly trained birds of prey to intimidate and scare off nuisance birds from an area.

Since the dawn of time prey species have been hard-wired to avoid predators. In falconry-based bird abatement, the predator is the falcon and the prey is any type of nuisance bird in the area.

When a falcon is seen hunting it naturally causes all prey species of birds to avoid the area due to fear of being eaten. If an area becomes known as a hunting grounds for raptors the nuisance birds will avoid it and look for new locations to feed/roost/live.

To effectively address bird issues, we have 13 highly trained falcons at our disposal, allowing us to form three teams and provide extensive coverage for projects across the United States.

We offer a daily rate for our falconry-based bird control services, and we are committed to tailoring a customized solution to meet your specific needs.

The falconer goes through extensive training to create a partnership with his falcons.

Both have their own jobs in the field:

  • The falconer locates and identifies staging and problem areas for invading flocks of nuisance birds.
  • The falconer flies falcons in those areas to deter nuisance birds from entering the property by using a lure to guide the falcon and simulate a hunt for food.
  • The falconer will always be on the lookout for nuisance birds entering the property and will release a falcon to drive them off the property.

At Sky Predators Bird Abatement, we pride ourselves on delivering top-quality services, and we are eager to provide our expert falconry-based solutions to address any bird-related challenges at you are facing.
