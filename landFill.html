Landfill Services

Nuisance birds in landfills are not only a problem for the landfill but for residents close to the landfill as well.


Birds carry things they find away from the landfill and cause trash to be spread to surrounding communities, as well as unsightly evidence of the bird presence.



Before Sky Predators

Gulls swarming around a landfill even as heavy equipment is working the area.




The Sky Predators Effect

When a falcon is released to pursue the gulls, the gulls immediately realize the danger and start to panic.




After Sky Predators

The use of falcons in the area will push gulls away from the food source. However, once the falcon is no longer present, the gulls will return because of the constant food source.


We can provide a solution the meets your needs by creating a re-occurring schedule to prevent a large scale problem.
