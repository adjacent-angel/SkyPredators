Landfill Services

Nuisance birds in landfills are not only a problem for the landfill but for residents close to the landfill as well.


Birds carry things they find away from the landfill and cause trash to be spread to surrounding communities, as well as unsightly evidence of the bird presence.



Before Sky Predators

Gulls swarming around a landfill even as heavy equipment is working the area.




The Sky Predators Effect

When a falcon is released in the area, the gulls immediately realize the danger and start to panic.




After Sky Predators

After the falcon is spotted consecutive days "hunting" in the area, the gulls move on and stay away from the area.


However, unless the presence of a falcon is consistent, the gulls will eventually return due to the abundance of food in the area.


We can provide a solution the meets your needs by creating a re-occurring schedule to prevent a large scale problem from returning
